Welcome to Seattle!

Seattle is a city of beautiful scenery, great food, and what are sure to be lasting memories.

Best things to do in Seattle

  1. Go sight-seeing at the Space Needle.
  2. Contribute to the Gum Wall at Pike Place Market.
  3. Take a ride to Bainbridge Island on the Seattle Ferries.
  4. Appreciate the art in the Olympic Sculpture Park.

Places to eat at Pike Place Market

  • Daily Dozen Doughnut Company
  • Piroshky Piroshky
  • Beecher's Cheese
  • The Original Starbucks
